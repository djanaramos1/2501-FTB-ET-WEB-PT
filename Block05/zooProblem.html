Lions

  • Mella

    • Mella is a Congo lion. She is yellow. She has been with the zoo for three years. She is very protective of Karl.

  • Karl

    • Karl is a Southwest African Lion. He is yellow with a large golden yellow mane with streaks of black in it. He is ten years old and has been with the zoo for 8 years.The life span of a male lion is 8 to 10 years. Karl is considered elderly and you will notice he is not as energetic as Mella.
